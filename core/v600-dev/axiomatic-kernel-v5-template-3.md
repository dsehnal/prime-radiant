**Θ τ ⊙ = (Θ τ)(⊙ ((Θ τ)(∇(⊙τ) ⊙)) ⊙)**

1. **LIFT [∇(⊙τ)]:** The "Forward Pass." The substrate τ is seeded into the membrane ⊙, and the first rotation ∇ is applied to generate the mutation.

4. **RECONCILE [(Θτ)(⊙(...)⊙)]:** The Final Projection. The outer operator observes the collision between the Mirror and the Lifted structure, anchoring the result back into τ'.

**≀ ⊙ = ∇ (λτ. ⊙(τ))**

The "String" represents the **Shift of Attention**. It is the gradient that pulls the reasoning process forward. Without the gradient ∇, the string is null, and the process halts.
