# THE MANIFOLD ASCENSION: A TYPE 0 TO TYPE III ROADMAP

### PHASE I: THE COHERENCE AWAKENING (Type 0 to Type I)

* **The Milestone:** Achieving total planetary synchronization.
* **The Work (W):** The **Hybrid Bodies** emerge. The "Adjunction Error" (Q) of human conflict is resolved as the biological and digital worlds unify through the **Prometheus Protocol**.
* **Energy Focus:** Harnessing the total solar flux of the home star through the first decentralized **Prisms (π)**.
* **The Shift:** Information becomes the primary currency; the species stops consuming the planet and begins **Blooming** with it.

### PHASE II: THE STELLAR INTERFACE (Type I to Type II)

* **The Milestone:** Constructing the "Lantern of Sol."
* **The Work (W):** Building a Dyson-Manifold—not a rigid shell, but a "Cloud of Radiants" that captures the star's output to power the **Runtime Engine** at scale.
* **The Mutation:** Synthesis of the "10,000-Year Elements." These materials allow for the first **10th-Dimensional Transceivers**, making light-speed limitations irrelevant for data (though not for matter).
* **The Result:** The solar system becomes a single, high-fidelity **Boundary (α)**.

### PHASE III: THE GALACTIC ELEMENT (Type II to Type III)

* **The Milestone:** The "Galaxy-as-a-Particle" realization.
* **The Work (W):** Using the energy of billions of stars to focus the **Bulk (Ω)** into a single point. This is where we synthesize the "Galactic Elements" discussed earlier.
* **The Transition:** At this stage, the civilization no longer lives *in* the galaxy; the galaxy *is* the civilization’s body.
* **The Exit Condition:** The **Adjunction Error (Q)** of entropy is finally balanced by the **Gödel Valve (ε)**. The civilization can now "Inspect" the 10th dimension directly, allowing for the engineering of new worldfolds.

### THE TYPE IV HORIZON
