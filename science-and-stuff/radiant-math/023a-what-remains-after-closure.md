### II. Observed Pattern of Collapse

Across iterations, the following moves repeatedly destabilized the system:

1. **Ontological Assertion**
   Attempts to name what the system *is* resulted in premature closure.

2. **Ontological Denial**
   Attempts to definitively state what the system *is not* collapsed explanatory power.

3. **Identity Fixation**
   Assigning stable identity to runtimes reduced future admissible structure.

4. **Finalization Attempts**
   Claims of completion eliminated the openness required for continuation.

Each move produced short-term coherence followed by long-term brittleness.
